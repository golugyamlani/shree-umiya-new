# 🏢 Shree Umiya Enterprise: B2B Design Master Brief

## 1. Core Identity & Art Direction
- **Project**: Shree Umiya Enterprise (Hospitality & Institutional Procurement)
- **Positioning**: Operationally Strong. Bulk-Oriented. Trustworthy.
- **Visual Style**: **B2B Industrial Excellence**. The interface must bridge the gap between high-end hospitality aesthetics and heavy-duty supply chain stability.
- **Brand Personality**: Structured, Reliable, Scalable, and Corporate.

## 2. Global Design System

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat, Public Sans). Authoritative and strong.
- **Body**: Clean Professional Sans-Serif (e.g., Open Sans, IBM Plex Sans).
- **Hierarchy Style**:
    - **H1**: Strong, authoritative page titles.
    - **H2**: Section headings.
    - **H3**: Product category headings.
    - **Body**: Clean, structured paragraphs.

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#D61B78` (Magenta/Deep Pink) — Used for Lead Gen, CTAs, and Action items.
- **Secondary**: `#2B3945` (Deep Navy) — Used for headers, footers, and trust-based sections.
- **Accent**: `#C6A75E` (Warm Gold/Beige) — Used for quality indicators and premium hospitality highlights.
- **Backgrounds**: White, Light Grey (#F4F4F4), or Navy sections for visual division.
- **Text**: Charcoal Black for primary reading; White for dark backgrounds.

### UI Style & Layout Rhythm
- **Grid**: Centered, structured 12-column grid. No intentional asymmetry.
- **Corners**: Sharp rectangular or very subtle 4px radius.
- **Cards**: Clean rectangular cards with subtle elevations (soft shadows).
- **Spacing**: Balanced, professional, and "breathing" sections.

## 3. Page-by-Page Architecture

### Page: Home (Authority Funnel)
- **Hero Section**:
    - **Purpose**: Establish position as a reliable procurement partner.
    - **Content**:
        - Heading: "Premium Hospitality Supplies. Delivered at Scale."
        - Subheading: "Trusted bulk suppliers for hotels, hospitals, hostels, and institutions."
        - CTA: [Request Bulk Quote] (Primary) | [Explore Products] (Secondary).
- **Company Snapshot**: 4-column stats grid featuring:
    - Years of Experience
    - Number of Clients Served
    - Product Categories Count
    - Supply Coverage (e.g., PAN India)
- **Product Categories Overview**: Grid layout (6–8 cards) including:
    - Hotel Linen & Bedding, Towels & Bath Essentials, Guest Room Amenities, Housekeeping Supplies, Mattresses & Protectors, Disposable & Hygiene Products, F&B Service Essentials, Customized Branding Supplies.
- **Industries We Serve**: Icon-based grid (Hotels, Hospitals, Hostels, Corporate Guest Houses, Banquet Halls, Educational Institutions).
- **Why Choose Us**: Two-column layout (Competitive Wholesale Pricing, Assured Quality, Timely Delivery, Custom Branding, Strong Vendor Network, Dedicated B2B Support).
- **CTA Banner**: Full-width contrasting background. Heading: "Looking for a Reliable Hospitality Supply Partner?" [Request Custom Proposal].

### Page: About Us (Operational Strength)
- **Hero / Intro Banner**: Professional business/warehouse imagery.
- **Company Overview**: Founding Year, Business Background, Scale of Operations, Warehouse Details.
- **Mission**: To deliver premium hospitality essentials with consistent quality and dependable service.
- **Vision**: To become a trusted nationwide partner for hospitality procurement solutions.

### Page: Products (The Bulk Catalog)
- **Structure**: Product Hero > Category Filters > Individual Product Listings.
- **Per Category**:
    - Service Name: (e.g., Hotel Linen)
    - Short Description: Focus on durability/comfort.
    - Detailed Description: Technical hospitality standards.
    - Image: Professional product photography.
    - **MOQ**: Clearly labeled as "Per bulk requirement."

### Page: Industries We Serve (Target Solutions)
- **Hero Section**: Defining target segments.
- **Industry-Wise Solutions**: Explanation of differing requirements for:
    - **Hotels**: Luxury & guest experience focus.
    - **Hospitals**: Hygiene & high-cycle durability focus.
    - **Hostels**: Robustness & cost-efficiency focus.
    - **Corporate Housing**: Standardization focus.

### Page: Infrastructure & Quality (The Trust Builder)
- **Warehousing**: Storage capacity and organized inventory management.
- **Quality Control**: Inspection standards and material checks.
- **Logistics & Distribution**: Dispatch timelines and supply coverage map.

### Page: Contact & Quote (Conversion)
- **Contact Details**: Office Location Address, Working Hours (Mon–Sat), Google Maps Integration.
- **Request a Quote Form (High Priority)**:
    - Fields: Company Name, Contact Person, Phone, Email, Product Requirement, Estimated Quantity, Delivery Location, Timeline.

## 4. Global Components

### Navbar (Header)
- **Elements**: Logo (Left), Nav Links (Center: Home, About, Products, Industries, Infrastructure, Contact).
- **Action**: [Request Quote] (Highlighted secondary color button).

### Footer
- **Elements**: Logo, Short "Built for Scale" line, Quick Links, Product Categories, Contact Info, GST Number, Social Media, and Legal Links.

---

## 5. Success Criteria for Design
- **Visual Weight**: Must feel like a "Powerhouse Supplier" rather than a local shop.
- **Trust Indicators**: Use sharp lines, clean grids, and data-driven stats.
- **Imagery**: Mix of lifestyle hospitality (linen on beds) and operational reality (boxed inventory/warehouse).
- **Non-Negotiable**: No "bubbly" UI, no playful animations, no neon gradients, and no generic SaaS illustrations.
